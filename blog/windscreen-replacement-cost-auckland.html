Bus Windscreen Replacement Cost in Auckland

What Does Bus Windscreen Replacement Cost in Auckland?

Based on industry consensus and our own experience across Auckland, bus windscreen replacement typically ranges from $250 to $500 for common mini-buses. Larger coach windscreens and specialist curved glass can run higher, depending on the make, model and whether the glass is imported.

What Affects the Price?

  • Make and model — common mini-buses like the Toyota Coaster or Nissan Civilian have more readily available glass, which keeps the cost down. Rare or imported models may need to be sourced from overseas.
  • Glass type — laminated windscreens, heated rear glass, and privacy-tinted side glass all vary in price.
  • ADAS cameras — if your bus has a forward-facing camera for lane-keep or emergency braking, recalibration adds to the cost.
  • Insurance vs private — if you're claiming on insurance, you usually only pay your excess. We invoice the insurer directly.
  • Mobile fitting — we come to you anywhere in Auckland at no extra travel charge.

Side and Back Glass Costs

Side window replacement is typically $180–$350 per pane depending on size and bonding method. Rear back glass ranges from $200–$400 for most mini-buses.

How to Get an Accurate Estimate

The best way to get a firm price is to call us on 09 972-1091 with your bus make, model and year. We can confirm glass availability and give you a price on the spot — whether you're paying privately or going through insurance.

📌 Note: Your actual price may vary depending on the specific glass required, bonding complexity and any sensor recalibration. We always confirm the full price before we start.
